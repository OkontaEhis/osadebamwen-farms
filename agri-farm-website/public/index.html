Innovating Agriculture Through Insect Farming

Osadebamwen Farms is a registered agricultural enterprise in Nigeria, dedicated to pioneering sustainable and innovative solutions through insect farming.

Founded with a commitment to transforming agricultural practices in Nigeria and across Africa, Osadebamwen Farms operates at the intersection of agriculture, biotechnology, and environmental sustainability. We aim to empower farmers, agribusinesses, and communities with cost-effective, eco-friendly alternatives to conventional livestock feed and fertilizer.

SERVICES WE OFFER

Black Soldier Fly Larvae (BSFL) Production

Large-scale cultivation of BSF larvae for high-protein animal feed.

BSF-Based Organic Fertilizer

Production of frass (BSF manure) as a natural, nutrient-rich soil enhancer.

RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT

Continuous innovation in BSF breeding, bio-conversion techniques, and farm automation.

TRAINING & CONSULTANCY

Capacity-building programs for farmers, cooperatives, and agribusinesses on insect farming.
